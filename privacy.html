Privacy Policy / Gizlilik Politikası

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how MystAI.ai (“we”, “our”, “the Platform”) collects, uses, and protects your personal data in compliance with GDPR, KVKK, and CCPA standards.

2. Data We Collect

  • Information voluntarily provided (e.g., email, name when contacting support).
  • Usage data such as IP address, browser type, and interaction metrics (via Google Analytics).
  • Uploaded content (e.g., coffee cup, tarot selections, palm image, or dream text) for AI processing.

3. Purpose of Data Use

Your data is processed solely to:

  • Provide and improve AI-based fortune readings,
  • Enhance user experience and site functionality,
  • Ensure compliance with legal obligations and security standards.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process data based on your consent (GDPR Article 6.1.a), legitimate interest (GDPR Article 6.1.f), and necessary compliance obligations.

5. Data Storage & Retention

Personal data is stored securely on encrypted servers and retained only as long as necessary to provide the service. Uploaded images or text for readings may be automatically deleted after interpretation.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. Limited sharing occurs only with:

  • Payment processors (Paddle, Stripe, İyzico) for transactions,
  • Analytics providers (Google Analytics) to improve site performance.

7. Cookies & Tracking

MystAI.ai uses essential cookies and analytics cookies to monitor site performance. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings.

8. User Rights

Under GDPR / KVKK, you have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your data,
  • Withdraw consent at any time,
  • Request data portability,
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.

9. Security

We implement SSL encryption, secure data transmission, and role-based access controls to protect all personal data from unauthorized access or misuse.

10. International Transfers

Data may be processed on servers outside your country but always under adequate data protection safeguards compliant with GDPR standards.

11. Children’s Privacy

MystAI.ai does not knowingly collect data from individuals under 16 years of age. If such data is identified, it will be promptly deleted.

12. Updates to Policy

We may periodically update this policy. The latest version will always be available at mystai.ai/privacy.html.

13. Contact Information

For privacy-related inquiries, contact: support@mystai.ai
