1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how MystAI.ai uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and improve our services in accordance with GDPR and KVKK.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences and analyze how you interact with the platform.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality (security, language preferences, login, etc.).
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand user interactions (via Google Analytics).
  • Performance Cookies: Used to optimize site loading and response times.
  • Marketing Cookies: Only used if you accept, to show relevant promotions or updates.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies anytime through your browser settings:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

5. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies belong to trusted third parties, such as Google Analytics and payment processors (Paddle, Stripe, İyzico). These third parties are responsible for their own cookie policies.

6. Consent

By using MystAI.ai, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy. You may change or withdraw your consent at any time through our cookie settings banner.

7. Updates

This Cookie Policy may be updated periodically. The latest version will always be available at mystai.ai/cookies.html.
