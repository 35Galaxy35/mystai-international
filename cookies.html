Cookies Policy

1. What Are Cookies?

MystAI uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience, improve functionality, analyze usage, and ensure secure authentication. Cookies are small text files stored on your device to remember certain information.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for login, security, and core functions.
  • Preference Cookies: Save your language and user settings.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with MystAI.
  • Performance Cookies: Improve loading speed and responsiveness.

3. Third-Party Services

MystAI may use trusted third-party services, such as:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Analytics tools

These providers may set their own cookies in compliance with GDPR and CCPA.

4. Why We Use Cookies

  • To maintain secure sessions
  • To remember language preferences
  • To improve service performance
  • To understand user behaviour for future improvements

5. Managing Cookies

You can disable or delete cookies through your browser settings. However, turning off essential cookies may prevent MystAI from functioning properly, including login and reading history features.

6. Data Protection Compliance

MystAI follows international data protection standards:

  • GDPR (Europe)
  • KVKK (Türkiye)
  • CCPA (California, USA)

7. Contact

If you have questions regarding cookies, contact us at:
Email: support@mystai.ai
